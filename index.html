Mission of my life

To be kind to others and myself.

Vission

The problem is, most of us concentrate on being kind to others but all too often we forget to be kind to ourselves.

Life's tough enough, stressful enough, hard enough, challenging enough, so there's no reason not to begin with myself.

To be kind to others and myself, I am trying to do some important tasks.I will mention some of them in below:

  • I treat others the way I want to be treated.
  • I always try to treat others with respect.
  • I always try to be my own best friend.
  • I try to express myself courageously.
  • I always try to be true to my word.
  • I always try to reinvent myself.
  • I always try to help others.
